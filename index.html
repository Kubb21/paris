    Hi, I am Sam Walters

    I hail from Wisconsin located on the west side of Lake Michigan. I highly recommend making a trip to Wisconsin at some point in time, read more about Wisconsin. You will be shocked what you might find in Wisconsin. Some of my favorite hobbies are juggling, playing mandolin, skiing, exercising, cooking, knitting, suduko, reading, drinking coffee, learning as much as I want to;) I try to be very 'conscience' of what I do and say. I do my best to tell the truth, and live my life with all of its struggles, but man, I am surely blessed when I acheive happiness.

    I am grateful to have met so many wonderful people. I gain a metaphorical souviner from each person, for example when you eat a grapefruit enjoy the bitterness because life isn't always going to be so sweet. And my goal is to pass on something special to them. I love to travel because of all the unnecessary...(maybe necessary) struggles that occur while traveling. All of these lessons are due to my travels. Traveling the world is when my eyes widen the most from the encounters of immersing myself in each destination and their cultures.

    As many stories go it starts with a dream. I always had a strong inclination to travel, and my first thoughts of travel is when I discovered people backpack through Europe. This was an image that I wanted to be painted into. I was 18 years old in 2015, and finally, I took steps to make traveling a passion of mine. I moved to Athens, Georgia to live with my oldest brother, Tygh. It was a great experience that fueled me to continue with my travel dreams. During my time in Georgia, I would call my best friend, Hope, she was back home in Wisconsin, and we would discuss plans to go on our first trip. We chose Hawaii, a dream of ours since we became friends 8 years ago. This was a huge realization in my life understanding if I have enough passion IT WILL HAPPEN. My passion for travel has only grown with each experience. I have been continously traveling with breaks in between since 2015. Find out more about each of my desitinations, and how I have made travel work for me. I hope you enjoy my stories and will get a metaphorical souviner out of each one. I always travel with a purpose in hopes of growing to be a better human being. Welcome!(firm handshake)
